About UtilityForge

UtilityForge is a free collection of online utilities built for developers, students, writers, and everyday professionals. Whether you need to count words, format JSON, generate a secure password, or convert a Unix timestamp — we have a tool for that, and it runs entirely in your browser.

Our Mission

We believe that useful tools should be free, fast, and private. Every tool on UtilityForge runs directly in your browser using client-side JavaScript. This means your data never leaves your device — nothing is uploaded, stored, or shared with any server. What you type stays with you.

What We Offer

UtilityForge provides tools across three main categories:

Text Tools

Count words, characters, and lines. Reverse, sort, and capitalize text. Convert case, remove duplicates, and clean up blank lines — all in seconds.

Developer Tools

Format and validate JSON, decode JWT tokens, test regular expressions, and convert Unix timestamps to human-readable dates. Essential tools for everyday development work.

Generators

Generate strong passwords, UUID lists, random numbers, random strings, colors, hex codes, and Lorem Ipsum text. Useful for development, testing, and design.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

At UtilityForge, your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you visit our website and use our online tools.

1. Tool Data

All tools on UtilityForge run entirely in your browser using client-side JavaScript. Any text you enter into a tool is never sent to our servers. We do not store, log, or retain any input you type into any tool. Your work stays on your device.

2. Account Information

If you choose to create a free account, we collect:

  • Your email address
  • Your name (optional, provided at signup)
  • Your subscription plan status (free or Pro)

This information is stored securely using Supabase, our authentication and database provider. We use this data solely to manage your account and subscription. We never sell your personal data to third parties.

3. Cookies & Local Storage

UtilityForge uses cookies and browser local storage in the following ways:

  • Authentication: To keep you signed in across sessions.
  • Plan cache: To remember your subscription plan locally for faster page loads.
  • Advertising cookies: We use Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this and other websites.
  • Analytics cookies: We may use analytics services to understand general usage patterns. This data is aggregated and anonymous.

You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ad Settings.

4. Google AdSense

We participate in the Google AdSense program, which allows Google to display advertisements on our pages. Google may use cookies and web beacons to collect information about your visits to this and other websites in order to serve you relevant advertisements.

You may opt out of personalized ads by visiting aboutads.info. For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy.

5. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those sites. We encourage you to review their privacy policies.

6. Children's Privacy

UtilityForge does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.
